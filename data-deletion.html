Data Deletion

Last updated: July 2026

myShakeEat has no accounts and no server. All data the app produces (history, preferences, recent picks) is stored only on your own phone — we hold no data of yours to delete.

Delete data inside the app

  • Clear history: open the app → Settings → History → the delete button at the top right clears all history. You can also swipe left on a single entry to delete it.
  • Clear all data: go to your phone's Settings → Apps → myShakeEat → Storage → Clear data, or simply uninstall the app, to remove all local data.

Third parties

The app uses Google AdMob for ads; data tied to the advertising identifier is handled by Google. You can reset or delete your advertising identifier under your phone's Settings → Privacy → Ads. See the Google Privacy Policy.
